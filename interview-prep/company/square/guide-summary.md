### Final Preparation Tips

1. **Practice explaining concepts out loud** — interviewers care about communication clarity.
2. **For every tool/pattern, be ready to explain when NOT to use it.**
3. **Always relate answers to real projects you've worked on.**
4. **Know the difference between "textbook answer" and "production answer"** — product companies value real-world experience.
5. **Be honest about what you don't know** — then explain how you'd figure it out.
6. **Prepare 2-3 project stories** with specific technical challenges and solutions.
7. **Know your numbers** — response times, query counts, user counts from your projects.
8. **Stay current** — mention Laravel 11 features, PHP 8.2+ features, modern tools.
